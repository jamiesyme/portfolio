About

Hi, I'm Jamie. I'm a software developer that enjoys exploring ideas through code.

When I started coding, I wanted to create games. Shortly after I started, however, I noticed that my projects naturally began to drift away from games: I was creating game engines, and then rendering engines, and then I was researching scripting language design, and then... I realize now that I never really wanted to create games, but rather, I wanted to create. I wanted to take ideas and turn them into reality. So that's what I'm trying to do now.

I'm currently attending the University of the Fraser Valley, and I'll be graduating in December 2018 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems. Until then, my plan is continue exploring ideas in my own projects, and gaining as much experience as possible. To see my progress, check out my resume.
