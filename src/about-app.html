About

Hi, I'm Jamie. I'm a software developer that enjoys exploring ideas through code.

When I started coding, I wanted to create games. Shortly after I started, however, I noticed that my projects naturally began to drift away from games: I was creating game engines, and then rendering engines, and then I was researching scripting language design, and then... Well, I realize now that I never really wanted to create games, but rather, I wanted to create. I wanted to take ideas and turn them into reality. So that's what I'm trying to do now (mostly through web development).

Right now, I'm attending the University of the Fraser Valley, and I'll be graduating in December 2018 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Information Systems. Until then, my plan is to continue exploring ideas in my own projects, and to gain as much work experience as possible. To see my progress, check out my resume.

I'm currently taking freelance work, so if you have a project that needs building, feel free to contact me here.
